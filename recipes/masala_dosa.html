Masala Dosa

Description

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a crispy fermented crepe made from rice batter and black lentils, filled with a spicy mashed potato filling. It is often served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Masala Dosa is a savory breakfast dish that is widely enjoyed across India. The dosa batter is spread thin on a hot griddle and cooked until crisp. The potato filling is prepared separately with a mix of spices, onions, and green chilies, and then placed inside the dosa before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
